CIMB Classics

UI • 2013

Summary

One of the earliest works that I did at VLT Labs was a pitching project for a golfing event by the CIMB Bank Malaysia. As the main organiser of the event, CIMB has full creative control over the marketing, and was the primary client that we were pitching to.

The primary goal was to sell event tickets by generating hype around the event. With that in mind, I worked with my awesome designer-mentor, Izwan Ismail, on the idea and art direction of our pitch.

Content first

To build up interest among visitors, we dedicated more than 80% of the front page for contents. Big, sensational news gets surfaced above-the-fold, while more frequent real-time updates (pulled from the organiser's Twitter account) sits just below.

More than 80% of the page is content

Easy ticketing

Ticketing information was intentionally receded to emphasise on content, but still easily discoverable. Showing both pricing and event details up-front allows for easy comparison for the more price-conscious visitors.

A palette that blends the organiser's and main sponsor's brand harmonically.

The front page with a prominent highlight of the main event. Green, grassy images was heavily used as a way to blend the main organiser's colour with Rolex's as the main sponsor.

Alternative exploration that pushed for more interactive contents to drive engagements.
