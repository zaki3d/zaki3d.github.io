As a kid growing up in Malaysia, I was exposed to art early on and quickly developed a fondness for the creative process. That fondness nurtured my current fascination with product design, which I approach with a user-centered thinking, striving to build products that are easy to use.

I’m currently working in Sydney for Atlassian as a Designer. As part of the Confluence team, I help design a tool to help thousands of our customers collaborate on documents online. We are also a growing design team, and are always looking for designers. If you’re interested, don’t be shy to reach out to us.

As I’m compiling my works, you might want to check out my occasional sketches on Instagram. You can also get in touch with me via Twitter or e-mail.
