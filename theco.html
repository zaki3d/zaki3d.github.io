The Co.

Identity, UI • 2014

One of The Co's co-working space in Duxton, Singapore. Image from The Co.

The Co. is a co-working space originally based in Singapore, but has since expanded into Malaysia. As part of the expansion, VLT Labs joined as a co-partner of the Malaysia-based spaces and was thus given full control and autonomy over the evolution of their branding.

As a designer in VLT Labs, I drove the exploration of the branding exercise, and collaborated with the envisioning of their new site.

Objective

With the expansion into Malaysia, The Co added 2 new location to their portfolio.
